# Who we're for?

## Target Audience Snapshot

For the restless souls trapped in sensible jobs. For the brands who'd rather die interesting than live boring. For everyone who looks at the creative industry and feels that itch under their skin - the one that whispers 'what if we broke everything and built something better?'

For everyone who's tired of pretending mediocre is marketable.

For the dreamers who got trapped in sensible jobs but never lost that wyrd spark.
