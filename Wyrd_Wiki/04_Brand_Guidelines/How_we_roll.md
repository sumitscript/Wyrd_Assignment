# How we roll

This is the stuff that makes us Wyrd—and makes it all work. They're the values we live by, hire by, fire by, and create by. They're how we know if we're doing it right.

- **See** (what others miss)
    - **The Obvious**
        - **THE BEST IDEAS SOUND INSANE.** Until they don't. Then they sound obvious.
        - **Safe ideas die alone.** Usually in meetings about meetings. Somewhere between "let's circle back" and "touching base.”
        - **Fancy words are where weak ideas hide.** Strip it naked, make it real, and let them shine.
        - The world needs another synergy presentation like it needs another LinkedIn influencer.
        - **The right answer isn't always the wyrd one.** Make them feel, make them think, make them remember.
        - Algorithms don't cry at movies or drunk-text their ex. They don’t feel, they don’t buy. **Stop making them your audience.**
    - **The Hidden**
        - The world isn't suffering from lack of content. **It's suffering from creative constipation.**
        - Data tells you what worked yesterday. Gut tells you what works tomorrow. **We trust both, but gut gets the corner office.**
        - **STORY ABOVE ALL:** Metrics matter, but wyrd works wonders. If your analytics tell the whole story, you're writing the wrong book.
        - Want engagement? Make them feel something- their spreadsheets can't measure.
        - **Good is the enemy of unforgettable**. We aim for the latter.
    - **The Future**
        - **AI isn't the enemy; boring is.** While everyone's afraid of AI taking their jobs, we're giving AI the jobs nobody wanted anyway.
        - Robots are great at everything nobody remembers. Be great at everything nobody forgets. **Let the machines optimize. You humanize.**
        - While everyone's teaching AI to think like humans, we encourage humans to think like humans again.
        - **AI can copy anything except your wyrd.** That's your superpower.
- **Do** (what others won’t)
    - **The Wild**
        - The best work leaves you unsure whether to frame it or hide it. **Your wyrdest thought? Probably your best.**
        - **Obsession looks like madness until it works.** Then they call it genius.
        - Make work that would make your future self proud. **And slightly concerned.**
        
        DISCLAIMER: Creation without chaos is just admin and chaos without craft is just noise. But chaos with a plan? That’s what works.
        
    - **The Mistakes**
        - **Fear is just excitement forgetting to breathe. Remind it.** If you're not failing wyrdly, you're probably not trying anything interesting.
        - **Every "wow" moment started as "wtf?”**  Every breakthrough started as someone's "wait, that's stupid... unless?”. If you're not slightly embarrassed by some of your attempts, you're not trying hard enough.
        - **Good ideas need bad ideas.** No breakdowns, no breakthroughs.
    - **The Work**
        - **If it ain't a hell yeah,** it's a no.
        - Sure, we color outside the lines – **but we keep our crayons sharp.**
        - **Everyone obsesses over the 80%. Magic lies in 20%** that ****no one notices**.** It’s that glitch that everyone tries to fix. The trick isn't working harder - it's knowing which 20% that actually sticks.
        - Make every pixel, word, frame, and moment count. **If it doesn’t matter, it doesn’t belong.**
        - Life's too short for burnout: **Even your laptop knows when to sleep.**
        - Find your groove - **even if its offbeat.**
- **Be** (what others can’t)
    - **The Fire**
        - **Take the work seriously, never yourself.** One of these should challenge you; the other should ground you.
        - **Stay hungry for the unknown.** The familiar is already overcrowded.
        - **Question everything, especially your wins.** Success is just failure that got lucky.
        - Learn like you'll live forever. Create like you won't.
        - The most serious stuff happens when you're having too much fun to realize it's work.
    - **The Tribe**
        - Solo genius is cute. **But none of us is as wyrd as all of us.**
        - **Every voice matters**, especially the quiet ones with the big ideas.
        - **Your wins are our wins.** (Your coffee is sometimes our coffee too)
        - Share the spotlight. Spread the credit. **Remember who helped you get there.**
    - **The Soul**
        - **Appreciate good work**, especially when it's different from yours
        - Treat everyone like they might be your future boss (or your future friend)
        - **Stay organized enough to find your good ideas later.** Keep some structure (just enough to stay fearless)
        - **Plan ahead**, but be ready to improvise.
        - **Make stuff that makes other people want to make stuff.** Ideas should burn bright and spread fast.
        - **Listen to all ideas**, even the ones that seem impossible.
