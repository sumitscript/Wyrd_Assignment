# What is Wyrd?

Some people call us an agency. Others call us a production house. A few think we're a tech startup. They're all kind of right and all completely wrong. We're just Wyrd. And we're building stuff that's a little wyrdr.

Wyrd is an experimental lab where stories, brands, and technology collide.

Born in film. Evolved through brands. Growing into tech. (because sometimes you need to build your own tools when the usual ones don't cut it.) But always, always wyrd.

At our core, we're an ecosystem designed to nurture ideas, talent, and ventures that are too distinctive for traditional spaces.
